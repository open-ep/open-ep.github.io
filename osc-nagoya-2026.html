Our Talk

Electronic Paper
in the Linux Kernel

"Electronic paper integration into Linux kernel — Open-EP community's OSS activities and CRA compliance approaches"

Session #2296957

Part 1 25 min

Open-EP: a Taiwan-based OSS community for embedded e-paper. Hardware drivers, multi-platform support (Renesas, NXP, Raspberry Pi), Linux kernel contributions via Device Tree & DRM. Yocto, Ubuntu & Android integration.

Part 2 20 min

EU Cybersecurity Regulations & CRA compliance. CI pipelines, vendor BSPs vs upstream Yocto strategies — using the e-paper project as a real-world case study for secure, regulation-ready embedded Linux.
