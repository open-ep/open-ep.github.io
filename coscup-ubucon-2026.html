About the Event

COSCUP × UBUCON Asia

COSCUP is one of Asia's largest community-driven open-source conferences, held annually in Taipei. In 2026 it joins forces with UBUCON Asia — the regional gathering of the Ubuntu community — for a weekend of talks, workshops, and collaboration across the open-source world.
